2. The 6 User Personas & Pain Points

User Role Operational Environment Core Pain Point
1. PCU Staff (รพ.สต.) Field visits, outdoor, remote areas, low connectivity. Small screen, aging user base, weak internet.
2. Hospital Staff Hospital desk, high phone call frequency. Paperwork overload, loose coordinate chains on Line groups lead to missed data.
3. Case Manager Attending physician overseeing patient cases, hospital desk, clinical settings. Managing patient referrals, home visits, and coordinating care with PCU staff and Hospital Staff.
4. SCFC Officers Craniofacial specialized center, CMU. Incomplete referral data, highly complex surgical scheduling queues.
5. Patients & Families Remote rural homes, language barriers (ethnic groups). Forgets clinical appointments, lacks knowledge on specialized feeding care.
6. IT Administrators Server rooms, technical admin dashboard. Complex data compliance requirements (PDPA) & area-based authorization.
